Welcome to the World of Terrania. A land full of mystery and wonder, with much of its destiny left up to the wildest imaginations...


Fight your way to claim glory over the land and ascend the ladder to achieve blessings from Aldura, God of glory.

Nauvban Arena

A planet of ecological and evolutionary extremes, Nauvban features a lush Forest, bitingly cold Tundra, and mysterious Ruins. The diverse ecology means that Fighters will have to be well-prepared to deal with Natives with varying types of behavior.

Amderak Arena

Amderak is home to a triad of biomes; with the mineral-rich air pouring from thermal vents in the muggy Volcanic Caves, the vivid foliage and cooling breezes of the Floating Continent, and the confining corridors of the Dragon Altar.

Vapar Arena

Vapar is a planet of seafoam and salt air. Seabed caverns lie below the sun-warmed sands of the Coast, the briny air mingling with the odor of fishy decay is the home of deviant Oceanids.
